## 👨‍🔬 About My Research
- 🔭 **Core Focus:** Deeply engaged in **Semantic Segmentation** for remote sensing imagery.
- 🧠 **AI Paradigms:** Bridging traditional Deep Learning architectures with **Large Vision-Language Models (e.g., CLIPSeg)** to push the boundaries of zero-shot/few-shot spatial understanding.
- 🛠 **Engineering:** Passionate about building robust AI pipelines, from low-level geospatial data processing to user-friendly **GUI software development**.
- 🌱 **Currently Exploring:** The intersection of multi-modal foundation models and massive Earth observation data.
